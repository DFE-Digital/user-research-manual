Peer advice and assuring your research

We’re developing a framework of professional standards for user research, and a single department-wide peer review and assurance process for user research. See our profession roadmap.

Until then, use the advice below.

It’s important that you know that you are doing a good job, and that your team and stakeholders can be confident that our work meets and exceeds standards.

As well as meeting the service standard, your research must always be ethical, legal and safe. Use the standards in this website where they have already been developed. Until then, you should aim to meet other existing industry standards, like those of the Market Research Society.
