Training and online resources

Beta This content has been written by our user research community, and is in beta. It is likely to change over time.

Your feedback and ideas will help develop it

Training budgets sit in your portfolio or business area. If you have identified a training course you’d like to take (using the user researcher capability framework to identify your development needs, and with the support of your line manager, profession manager, or mentor) then speak to your line manager about how funding works.

Introduction to user research in government

The cross-government Understanding users and their needs training course is a good introduction to conducting user research in the context of government.

You can join the Training working group and help them do this.
